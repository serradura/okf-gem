# Shapely

A bundle shaped for the structural evidence views: a hub in `core/` that two
areas link into (the `graph --hubs` ranking), a tag spread across areas and
tags local to one (the totals `tags --by` carries).

# Areas

* [Billing](billing/) - the renewal flow.
* [Core](core/) - the shared record every flow converges on.
* [Flows](flows/) - the activate/suspend pair.
